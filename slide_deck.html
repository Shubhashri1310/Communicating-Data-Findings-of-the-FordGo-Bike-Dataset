Fordgo Bike System Dataset 2018

by Shubhashri Chavan

Investigation Overview

  • Fordgo Bike is a public bike sharing system that provides bike service in San Francisco. Overall 1863721 entries were gathered from the FordGo Bike dataset belonging to the year 2018 to carry out the analysis. It contains the trip data about all the bike riders as the bike_id, start_station,end_station and many other fields. Furthermore, it also preserves the data about the bike riders as-in the type of user i'e., customer or subscriber, gender and birth_year.

Dataset Overview

  • Monthly data of 2018 was collected and merged together to form the master dataset for carrying out analysis. The data was cleaned and the outliers affecting the visualizations were stripped off for a clean visualization. The graphs had to go through some tweaks for observing the data closely. Various trends in the data were observed during the exploration analysis phase which later led to communicate the major findings of the data.

Subscribers :¶

The subscribers count seems bulging during the morning hours 8-9 (maximum at 8 a.m). The violin plot goes flattening post 10 a.m till 3 p.m. and again starts swelling out till 5.30 p.m(maximum at 5 p.m) which pretty much suggests that they are working professionals who hire bikes for going to and from home for work. A noticable aspect here is that on weekends there is quite a less frequency of bikes being hired by the subscribers both on 9 in the morning or 5 in the evening. The reason being that they are tired working whole week and want to get some rest on weekends.¶

Customers :¶

The frequency of bikes being hired by customers in office hours is fairly less on weekdays whereas, they have maximum bike hiring post 5 pm on the weekdays probably to collect vegetables from the market or some other work. The customers on weekends and prefer going out post 12 in the afternoon for roaming in and around the city on weekdays.¶
